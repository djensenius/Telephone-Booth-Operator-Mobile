Telephone Booth Operator is the operator console for a private, self-hosted
art-installation server (the Telephone-Booth Operator). In normal use it
signs in with OAuth + PKCE and connects to the operator's own server, which
is not publicly accessible.

To let you review the complete interface without any account or server, the
app ships with a built-in DEMO MODE backed by bundled sample data:

  1. Launch the app.
  2. On the sign-in screen, tap the "Try Demo Mode" button.
  3. The full operator console loads with realistic sample data — dashboard,
     statistics, call sessions, messages, questions, and system views.

Demo mode makes no network requests. There are no in-app purchases, ads, or
third-party tracking. The app is a niche tool for the developer's own art
installation and is not intended for general consumer audiences.
